Why We Built Ignitus

Not every child gets into a top college. But every child deserves to see what's possible.

At Ignitus, we believe education begins with exposure — not just to marks, but to the world. We built Ignitus to give every student the chance to think freely, build boldly, and prepare meaningfully for what lies beyond school.

This isn't about coaching for a test. It's about preparing to take on the world with confidence, clarity, and curiosity. We didn't want to build another coaching center. We wanted to build a movement.

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

What is the ideal age group for Ignitus? +

Ignitus is designed for students aged 13-18 who are curious about the world and ready to build something meaningful. Our programs are tailored to different skill levels and interests.

How are classes conducted? +

We use a blended approach with live online sessions, 1:1 mentorship, project-based learning, and peer collaboration. Students get both structured guidance and freedom to explore.

Is Ignitus right for beginners? +

Absolutely! Our programs are designed to meet students where they are. Whether you're a complete beginner or have some experience, we'll help you grow and build confidence.

Do you help with global admissions? +

Yes! We provide personalized guidance for global university applications, including portfolio development, storytelling, and showcasing your unique projects and achievements.
